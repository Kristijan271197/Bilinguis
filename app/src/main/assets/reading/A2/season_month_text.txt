There are 4 seasons in a year: winter, spring, summer and autumn. Every season is special. There are 12 months in a year: January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November and December. 

In December, January and February the weather is cold. The days are short, and the nights are long and dark. Almost every day it is snowing. December is the first winter month, next comes January. January is the coldest month in our town. It is freezing sometimes.

In March, April and May the weather is warm. Everything is green and beautiful. The nights are shorter. There are a lot of flowers in spring. Spring is my favorite season of the year, and April is my favorite month. 

In June, July and August the weather is hot. The days are long, and everyone is spending their time on the beach. In July and August people usually go on a vacation. 

In September, October and November the weather is cloudy and rainy. The leaves are falling from the trees. Everything is yellow and brown. Autumn is also chilly and fogy.
